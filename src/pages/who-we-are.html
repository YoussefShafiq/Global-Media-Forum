Who We Are?

The Egypt Media Forum (EMF) is an annual and independent international platform for media development based in Cairo. We advance know-how, policy, and capacity building, and facilitate professional exchange throughout Egypt and the MENA region.

We work on developing the media work environment in Egypt and the MENA region. The Forum runs throughout the year as an international platform for development in the form of sustainable products that aim to develop media tools. These include a press training unit and a digital platform providing advanced knowledge and utilizing in-depth studies.

Mission

EMF is creating an annual hub for Egyptian media and its international counterparts, focusing on diversity and new content trends. We invite elite trainers and speakers from around the world to participate in innovative presentations, roundtables, and workshops to discuss the latest trends in the media industry.

Vision

We believe in development built on achievements—not by ignoring them. Media is an effective tool for supporting development. EMF aims to use the media as a community development tool and to enable knowledge transfer, policy development, and professional exchange across the region.
