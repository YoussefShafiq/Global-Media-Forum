Forum Languages

The primary language of the forum is Arabic, with English also used. Translation services between Arabic and English are available for most forum activities.

Attendance Requirements

Participation in forum activities requires prior registration and payment of applicable fees, which vary by category. Participation is subject to availability.

Categories and Fees

Category A

  • Egyptian journalists and media professionals.
  • Egyptian media and journalism researchers and academics.
  • Journalists and media professionals working for Egyptian media outlets of all nationalities.
  • Official spokespersons and media advisors for Egyptian ministries and public sector entities.
  • Journalism and media undergraduate students of all nationalities.

Registration Fee: Exempt

Category B

  • Non-Egyptian journalists and media professionals.
  • Non-Egyptian media and journalism researchers and academics.
  • Non-Egyptian official spokespersons and media advisors for public institutions.

Registration Fee: $250

Fees cover access to all forum activities and coffee/lunch breaks on both days. Additional costs are not included. Payment is due after registration and upon receipt of acceptance confirmation via email.

Category C

  • Egyptian and non-Egyptian corporate communicators, public relations, and related professionals in private companies, agencies, and institutions.
  • Egyptian and non-Egyptian marketing and advertising professionals.

Registration Fee: $500
